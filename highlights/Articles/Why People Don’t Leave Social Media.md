## Highlights
- The problem I have outlined can be summarized in two ways. It’s a problem of story, and a problem of community. It’s a problem of story because of plausibility—it sounds like exile, it sounds like being asked to embrace a narrative of exclusion for very abstract gains. Which is why it’s a problem of community: when community is only found via digital means, you need an alternative before you can consider leaving.
